About Me

Born on August 17th in 1998, I grew up here in Cape Town.


I was a student at Friends of Design but spend a lot of time helping my mom with her business; Sweetarts. I went to Timour Hall in my primary school years, went to Norman Henshilwood for grade 8 and 9, and Falsebay College for grade 10 to 12, before joining Friends of Design.


After joining Friends of Design I have picked up many skills in audio design, video editing, web design, as well as graphic design and although I have learned much, I still have a lot to learn...
